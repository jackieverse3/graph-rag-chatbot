Apple was founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in 1976.
Apple develooped the Macintosh Computer and later introduced the iPhone,
which runs on the iOS operating system. Google, founded by Larry Page and 
Sergey Brin in 1998, developed the Android operating system to compete
with iOS. Google also acquired YouTube in 2006 to expand its video services.

Microsoft, founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen, developed the Windows
operating system. Microsoft partnered with OpenAI, investing billions of
dollars to support artificial intelligence development. OpenAI, led by CEO 
Sam Altman, developed the popular conversational AI model known as 
ChatGPT. Microsoft integrated ChatGPT technology into its Bing search
engine to challenge Google's dominance in search.
